14//! # Ifá-Std - The 16 Odù Domains
15//!
16//! Standard library implementing the 16 principal Odù as Rust modules.
17//!
18//! ## Domain Overview
19//!
20//! | Binary | Odù | Purpose |
21//! |--------|-----|---------|
22//! | 1111 | Ọ̀gbè | System, CLI Args, Lifecycle |
23//! | 0000 | Ọ̀yẹ̀kú | Exit, Sleep |
24//! | 0110 | Ìwòrì | Time, Iteration |
25//! | 1001 | Òdí | Files, Database |
26//! | 1100 | Ìrosù | Console I/O |
27//! | 0011 | Ọ̀wọ́nrín | Random |
28//! | 1000 | Ọ̀bàrà | Math (Add/Mul) |
29//! | 0001 | Ọ̀kànràn | Errors, Assertions |
30//! | 1110 | Ògúndá | Arrays, Processes |
31//! | 0111 | Ọ̀sá | Concurrency |
32//! | 0100 | Ìká | Strings |
33//! | 0010 | Òtúúrúpọ̀n | Math (Sub/Div) |
34//! | 1011 | Òtúrá | Networking |
35//! | 1101 | Ìrẹtẹ̀ | Crypto, Compression |
36//! | 1010 | Ọ̀ṣẹ́ | Graphics, UI |
37//! | 0101 | Òfún | Permissions, Reflection |
