Available Word Mappings

How it works: Each word in the code is mapped to its Yoruba and English equivalents. Click any highlighted word to switch it individually, or use the toggle buttons to switch all words at once.

Features

  • Individual Word Switching: Click any highlighted word to switch just that word
  • Global Language Toggle: Use Yoruba/English buttons to switch all words
  • Visual Feedback: Words are color-coded (blue for Yoruba, orange for English)
  • Hover Effects: Hover over words to see interactive feedback
  • Smooth Animations: Words animate when switching
  • Comprehensive Mappings: Covers keywords, operators, domains, and more
